Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a market leader

Founded in 2010 by Ronald and Victoria Nsubuga, RonVick Gas Service Limited started as a small family business with a single delivery truck and a vision to provide reliable gas solutions to the Kasana Luwero community.

What began as a modest operation has grown into one of the most trusted gas suppliers in the region, known for our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve thousands of residential and commercial customers, offering a comprehensive range of gas products and services backed by our team of experienced professionals.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable gas solutions that power homes and businesses while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

Our Vision

To be the preferred gas service provider in Central Uganda, recognized for innovation, safety, and exceptional customer experiences.

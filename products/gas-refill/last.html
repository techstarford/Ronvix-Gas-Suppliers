Frequently Asked Questions

How long does refilling take?

Standard refilling takes 2‑4 hours. With pickup and delivery, we aim to deliver within 24 hours of booking.

Do you refill all brands of LPG cylinders?

Yes! We refill all major brands including Shell, Total, Oryx, Stabex, Hass, Hashi, Mogas, Meru, and others.

Is there a delivery charge?

Pickup and delivery service costs UGX 5,000. Refill‑only (you bring cylinder) is free.

What if my cylinder fails the safety check?

We’ll inform you immediately and recommend repair or replacement options for your safety.

Can I schedule regular refills?

Yes! We offer subscription plans for regular automatic refills. Contact us for details.

Do you provide emergency refills?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency refill service. Call 0788971271 for immediate assistance.

Product Description

The RonVick Heavy Duty 50kg Gas Cylinder is engineered for demanding industrial and commercial applications. Built with high-grade steel and featuring a BS341 No.1 valve, this cylinder meets all international safety standards while providing reliable performance.

Key features include:

  • Reinforced steel construction for maximum durability
  • Advanced safety valve with overpressure protection
  • Corrosion-resistant coating for long lifespan
  • Ergonomic handles for easier transportation
  • Standard industrial color that maintains professional appearance

Ideal for restaurants, hotels, manufacturing facilities, and other high-volume gas users. Each cylinder undergoes rigorous testing to ensure safety and reliability.

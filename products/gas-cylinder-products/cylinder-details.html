Safety Information & Guidelines

Important: Always follow safety guidelines when using gas cylinders. Improper use can lead to fire, explosion, or serious injury.

Storage Guidelines

  • Store cylinder in well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight
  • Keep cylinder upright at all times - never lay it down
  • Store away from heat sources, stoves, or electrical appliances
  • Do not store in basements or below ground level
  • Keep out of reach of children and pets

Usage Guidelines

  • Always check for leaks using soap water solution before use
  • Use only certified regulators and hoses
  • Turn off cylinder valve when not in use
  • Never use damaged or rusty cylinders
  • Keep burner flames adjusted to blue, not yellow

Emergency Procedures

  • If you smell gas, immediately turn off cylinder valve
  • Do not operate electrical switches or create sparks
  • Open doors and windows for ventilation
  • Call emergency: 0788971271
  • If fire occurs, use dry powder extinguisher - NOT water

Regular Checks

  • Check cylinder for rust or damage monthly
  • Inspect hose and regulator for cracks or wear
  • Ensure valve cap is always in place when not connected
  • Have cylinder pressure tested every 10 years
  • Keep cylinder certification and test dates recorded

Warning Signs - Do Not Use If:

Visible rust or corrosion
Dents or physical damage
Valve is damaged or leaking
Past test date (10 years)
Missing safety labels
Unusual smell when valve is closed

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for this cylinder?

This Shell gas cylinder comes with a 5-year manufacturer warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty covers the cylinder body and valve system. Proof of purchase is required for warranty claims.

How often should I refill this 13kg cylinder?

For a family of 3-4 persons using it for regular cooking, a 13kg cylinder typically lasts 4-6 weeks. For smaller households or lighter usage, it can last 8-10 weeks.

Do you provide installation service?

Yes! We provide free professional installation with every cylinder purchase. Our certified technician will install the cylinder, connect it properly, and perform a safety check.

What should I do if I smell gas?

Immediately turn off the cylinder valve, open windows for ventilation, do not operate electrical switches, and call our emergency line: 0788971271.

How do I know when the cylinder needs testing?

Gas cylinders should be pressure tested every 10 years. The test date is stamped on the cylinder body. We can arrange testing for you when it's due.
